Chicken Sandwich

Description

Chop up some lettuce and roast a chicken breast, add both with some mayo between some bread for a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Chicken
  • Bread
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Roast chicken breast for 25/30 minutes in oven
  2. Chop letuce
  3. Wash lettuce
  4. Toast bread
  5. Add copious amounts of mayo to bread
  6. Add lettuce
  7. Add chicken
  8. Serve
