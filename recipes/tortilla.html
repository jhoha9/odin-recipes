Tortilla

Description

Chop up and fry some potatoes, soak them in whisked eggs and then fry. Flip the tortilla and toast both sides, then serve on a plate.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Chop up the potatoes into small cubes
  2. Fry the potatoes
  3. Beat together the eggs
  4. Soak the potatoes in the eggs for thirty minutes
  5. Add the mixture to the frying pan
  6. After two minutes, flip the tortilla with a plate and fry the other side
  7. Serve
